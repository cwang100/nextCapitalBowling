Introduction

To create new user, click sign up button. To log in, click log in button. To add new bowler, click add new bowler button. To show all bowler, click show all bowler button. To add new league, click add new league button. To add player to league, select a league and add at the bottom of the league detail. To view lotteries, click view lotteries after select a league. Tickets can be purchaced and winners can be drawn from each lottery.
